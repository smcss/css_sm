Groups

Groups — are cornerstone of whole system. Groups is a way to control space between several adjacent elements. Groups can be of two types: group of blocks and group of inline elements. Also they are classified by method used: padding groups or margin groups. By default margin groups are advised to use. And only in some rare cases — padding groups should be used instead.
